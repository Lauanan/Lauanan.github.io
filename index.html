About 关于我

My research centers on safety issues in LLMs and agentic AI. I am particularly interested in how guard mechanisms can be designed to constrain risky behaviors, improve controllability, and support more trustworthy deployment of advanced AI systems. My recent interests include Agent Guard, LLM Guard, runtime protection, and governance-based defense strategies. 我的研究聚焦于大语言模型与 Agent 系统中的安全问题。我尤其关注如何通过 guard 机制约束高风险行为、增强系统可控性，并支撑更可信的高级 AI 系统部署。近期重点包括 Agent Guard、LLM Guard、运行时保护以及基于治理思想的防御策略。
